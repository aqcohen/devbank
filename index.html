What is Developers Bank?

We are a joint initiatitive between Engineering Faculty and System Engineering Program to close the job gap between our students and graduates, and local, national and international companies developers needs.

We works as a social network where students and graduates profiles are ranked, and the companies look for the best talent that fits its projects.
